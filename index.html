How to Play

You have 6 tries to guess the correct word. The colour of the tiles will change to reflect how close your guess was to the answer. Once per week the five letter word will be in Irish. The word will not contain a fada. You will notice a saying 'As Gaeilge Inniu', to let you know when you should guess as gaeilge.

A

The correct letter

N

The correct letter in the wrong place

Y

The incorrect letter
